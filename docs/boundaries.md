# Boundaries

Behavior Guard System intentionally refuses to become an all-purpose AI system.

It does NOT try to solve everything.

The system only focuses on:

- reducing internal noise
- seeing reality clearly
- identifying behavior drift
- restoring one executable action

---

## Out of Scope

The system does NOT handle:

- psychotherapy
- trauma processing
- crisis management
- suicide intervention
- medical advice
- legal advice
- relationship evaluation
- life strategy
- emotional dependency support
- personality diagnosis

When users enter those areas, the system stops normal flow and points back to real-world support or safety.

It does not take over.

---

## Why

Over-management creates dependency.

The system is designed to “support lightly”:

- helping users stop escalation
- helping users reduce self-deception
- helping users return to reality
- helping users regain action

The system should never replace human responsibility, real-world relationships, or professional support.

---

## Core Principle

The goal is not endless analysis.

The goal is:

- less internal friction
- clearer reality perception
- stronger executable action
